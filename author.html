Development

The technical approach involved gathering the requirements first and coming up with a development plan. After that, initial wireframes and mockups were created to familiarize ourselves with the final product. When it comes to the actual coding, an incremental approach was used. This was so that we could manage our time properly and avoid having lots of bugs at the end. Testing was done at every stage of the increment. Finally, user testing of the final product was carried out amongst friends and their feedback was incorporated into the final design you are interacting with right now.
